Terms & Conditions

By booking any service—whether in person, verbally, by phone, fax, email, online, or in writing—you acknowledge and accept the following terms and conditions set by KidsPartyLab – Mallorca.

Booking Process

To book our services, please fill out our website contact form. A booking is only confirmed once we receive your deposit and send you a confirmation via email or message.

Please note: all deposits are non-refundable. This policy supports the sustainability of our business by covering essential costs such as staffing, logistics, and operations. If you need to reschedule, we will always do our best to offer an alternative date, provided sufficient notice is given.

In the rare event that a specific service becomes unavailable due to unforeseen circumstances, KidsPartyLab – Mallorca reserves the right to substitute it with a similar option of equal value.

Booking Requirements & Information

  • A minimum 3-hour booking is required for babysitting services during parties and events.
  • All entertainers and babysitters are fully vetted and background-checked.
  • Pool activities require a certified lifeguard present—we do not provide water games without one.
  • Easy access to the party venue and available parking are required. Additional fees may apply for difficult access.
  • A transport fee will be charged based on travel distance.
  • A secure preparation area (off-limits to children) must be provided for entertainers.

Payment

A deposit is required to secure your booking—this amount will be clearly communicated during the booking process. The remaining balance is due on or before the day of your event.

Cancellations & Refunds

If you cancel part of a multi-service booking, 50% of the cancelled service(s) will be charged as a cancellation fee. We strongly recommend informing us as early as possible so we can explore alternative solutions.

In the rare case that we must cancel due to unforeseen circumstances, you will receive a full refund or the option to reschedule at no extra cost.

Changes to bookings

Need to change your booking? Please contact us before the deadline provided during the booking process. We'll do our best to accommodate your request, but all changes are subject to availability.

If you'd like to modify the details of your booked services—such as package inclusions—please get in touch early so we can confirm availability and plan accordingly.

Safety & Liability

The safety of all participants is our top priority. Please follow the safety guidelines and instructions provided by our team during your event.

KidsPartyLab – Mallorca is not liable for injuries, accidents, or damages that may occur. Should a participant cause damage to third-party property or equipment, they (or their guardian/client) are responsible for covering repair or replacement costs.

Clients must inform us of:

  • Any known medical conditions or allergies
  • Emergency contact information
  • Any dietary needs, health insurance details, required medications, sun protection, or refreshments

Force Majeure

In cases of natural disasters, government restrictions, or other events beyond our control that prevent us from delivering services, we reserve the right to reschedule or cancel with fair compensation.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. For full details on how we collect, use, and protect your personal data, please refer to our Privacy Policy page.
